The Project Idea

A vision turned into reality

The idea of "Smart University Advisor" came from the urgent need to guide students towards university majors suitable for their skills and interests, contributing to reducing confusion and increasing chances of success and excellence in academic and professional fields.

We believe that the right choice of university major is not just an academic decision, but the beginning of a journey towards self-realization and professional excellence. Therefore, we designed our system to be a comprehensive guide based on scientific and methodological foundations.

🎯

Project Goals

Our mission towards excellence

  • 1 Helping students discover their scientific and personal inclinations and abilities
  • 2 Guiding students towards suitable university majors based on scientific analysis
  • 3 Providing comprehensive information about university majors and labor market requirements
  • 4 Alleviating the burden on parents and academic advisors
  • 5 Raising the level of job and professional satisfaction among graduates
  • 6 Building a generation aware of its capabilities and qualified to face future challenges
